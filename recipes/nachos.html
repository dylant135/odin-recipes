Nachos

Description

These cheesy beef Nachos are very good, cheap, and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 thing of velvita cheese
  • taco bell seasoning
  • chips
  • can of rotel

Directions

  1. brown the beef
  2. add taco seasoning and follow directions on packet
  3. in another bowl melt velvita
  4. add in the rotel and beef
  5. put over chips and enjoy
