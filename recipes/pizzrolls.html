Pizza Rolls

Description

Everyone knows pizza rolls are good, but we stepped it up with these easy homemeade pizza rolls

Ingredients

  • Cresant rolls
  • Pizza sauce
  • Cheese
  • Peperoni

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Roll out rolls and put on sauce
  3. Add cheese and Peperoni
  4. Bake for 8 minutes and enjoy
